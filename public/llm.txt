## Common Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is Caberu HIPAA compliant?
A: Yes, absolutely. Caberu is fully HIPAA compliant with enterprise-grade encryption, secure data storage, comprehensive audit logs, and signed BAAs (Business Associate Agreements).

Q: How long does implementation take?
A: Most practices are operational within 48 hours, including calendar integration, AI training, and staff onboarding.

Q: What if the AI can't handle a call?
A: The AI handles 95%+ of routine calls, but can seamlessly transfer complex situations to staff with full conversation context.

Q: Can patients request a real person?
A: Yes, patients can always request to speak with staff and will be transferred immediately with full conversation context.

Q: What integrations are available?
A: Caberu integrates with major dental practice management systems (Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve) and offers API access for custom integrations.

Q: What languages are supported?
A: Currently English and Spanish, with automatic language detection. Additional languages in development.

Q: Can I customize the AI's responses?
A: Yes, full control over scripts, greetings, responses, and personality to match your practice's brand.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
